## 🎯 **DETAILED COMPETITOR ANALYSIS**

### **1. TENNIS AI (tennisai.net)**

#### **Strengths:**
- ✅ Autonomous AI agent (not just video analysis)
- ✅ All strokes analyzed (serve, forehand, backhand)
- ✅ Training plan feature with personalized drills
- ✅ Pro benchmark comparisons
- ✅ 2,000+ users across 25 countries
- ✅ B2B partnerships with 3,500 coaches
- ✅ Clean, minimalist UI

#### **Key Metrics They Track:**
- Racquet speed
- Ball toss
- Contact point
- Swing trajectory
- Professional comparison metrics

#### **Pricing:**
- Free: 2 analyses
- Premium: €15/month or €150/year
- B2B: Custom pricing for academies

#### **What We Should Copy:**
1. **Training Plan Generator** - AI creates structured improvement plans
2. **Drill Library** - Personalized drill recommendations based on gaps
3. **Pro Benchmark System** - Compare user metrics against ATP/WTA players
4. **Progress Tracking Dashboard** - Visual charts showing improvement
5. **B2B Academy Licensing** - White-label for tennis clubs
6. **Multi-stroke Support** - Serve, forehand, backhand, volley, overhead

### **2. SWINGVISION**

#### **Strengths:**
- ✅ 1M+ users (market leader)
- ✅ Patented AI line calling (97% accuracy within 10cm)
- ✅ Backed by Andy Roddick & Lindsay Davenport
- ✅ Apple Watch integration
- ✅ Auto-highlights with dead-time removal
- ✅ Professional-grade stats
- ✅ Built by Tesla & Apple AI experts

#### **Key Features:**
- **Shot tracking:** Speed, depth, accuracy, rally length
- **Line calling:** Real-time audio calls + slow-motion replay
- **Video editing:** Auto-trims dead time, creates highlight reels
- **Apple Watch:** Challenge line calls, start sessions via Siri
- **Cloud storage:** Unlimited for Pro users
- **Coaching:** Personalized post-session advice

#### **Pricing:**
- Free: 2 hours/month
- Pro: $150/year ($12.50/month)
- Features: Unlimited recording, line calling, cloud storage

#### **UI/UX Highlights:**
- Clean, professional design
- Real-time camera view on Apple Watch
- Shot-by-shot and point-by-point video review
- Stats overlay on videos
- One-tap line call challenges

#### **What We Should Copy:**
1. **Line Calling System** - Audio + visual feedback for in/out calls
2. **Auto-Highlights Generator** - Remove dead time, create reels
3. **Shot Speed Display** - Real-time mph/kph tracking
4. **Depth & Accuracy Maps** - Heat maps showing shot placement
5. **Rally Length Tracking** - Count shots per point
6. **Apple Watch App** - Wearable control and line challenges
7. **Video Overlay System** - Stats displayed directly on video
8. **Professional Endorsements** - Get pro player backing

## 🎨 **UI/UX BEST PRACTICES FROM COMPETITORS**

### **Common Design Patterns:**

#### **1. Modern Minimalist Design**
- Clean white/dark backgrounds
- Gradient accents (neon green, electric blue)
- Large, readable typography
- Spacious layouts
- High contrast

#### **2. Video-First Interface**
- Video player as primary element
- Stats overlay on video
- Scrubber with shot markers
- Slow-motion replay controls
- Picture-in-picture capability

#### **3. Dashboard Layouts**
- Card-based stat grids
- Progress charts prominently displayed
- Recent activity feed
- Quick action buttons
- Achievement displays

#### **4. Navigation Patterns**
- Bottom tab bar (iOS standard)
- Hamburger menu for settings
- Floating action button for recording
- Swipe gestures for navigation
- Pull-to-refresh

#### **5. Onboarding Flows**
- Simple 3-4 screen intro
- Permission requests with explanations
- Camera setup tutorial
- First analysis walkthrough
- Social connection prompts
