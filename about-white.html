The problem we solve

Built because feedback shouldn't cost a coaching session.

Most tennis players finish every session not knowing what actually cost them points — or how to fix it before the next one. The gap between filming a stroke and understanding what's wrong with it used to take days, a coach, and a lot of interpretation.

We built the AI film room that levels the playing field between coached and uncoached athletes. The translation problem between biomechanics data and real coaching language — solved. The 'progress feels invisible' problem — fixed with session-over-session tracking. The privacy and billing complexity — handled so athletes can focus on tennis.

The product is meant to support coaching and player development. It is not a substitute for medical advice, emergency response, or a guarantee of athletic results.

Our principles

The convictions behind every decision.

SmartSwing AI was built around a set of hard-held beliefs about what actually makes technology useful for sport.

01

Clarity beats completeness

A report with ten metrics and no clear action is worse than one with three priorities and an obvious next step. We always choose what's actionable over what's exhaustive.

02

Coaching is human. AI is a tool.

SmartSwing AI is built to support coaches, not replace them. Every report is designed to create better conversations — not bypass the people who know their athletes best.

03

Progress should be visible

Improvement that can't be measured feels invisible. Every session should produce evidence that something changed — and show which direction it moved.

04

Privacy is not a feature

Role-based access, scoped visibility, and plan-level guardrails are baked into the architecture — not bolted on afterward. Players own their data. Coaches see what they need.

05

Fairness in scoring

A 10-year-old junior and a 4.5 adult shouldn't be graded on the same scale. Level, age, and gender adjustments make feedback feel fair and keep motivation intact.

06

Zero friction to start

No app download, no complicated setup. If you have a phone and a browser, you can get a biomechanics report. The barrier should be a bad backswing, not a software install.
