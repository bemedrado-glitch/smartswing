About

Tennis improvement should feel specific, fast, and actionable.

SmartSwing AI helps players, parents, coaches, and performance programs turn one tennis video into a clearer next step. Instead of broad tips, the platform is built to identify movement patterns, summarize key mechanics, recommend drills, and keep progress in one place.

What SmartSwing AI is built to do

  • Translate video into readable biomechanics feedback and player-friendly priorities.
  • Shorten the loop between recording, understanding, and practicing.
  • Give players and coaches one place to review analysis, drills, tactics, and progress.
  • Support responsible growth with privacy, accessibility, and billing transparency.

Who it serves

SmartSwing AI is designed for junior athletes, adult improvers, competitive players, coaches, academies, and families who want a more structured training loop without needing a full custom technology stack.

The product is meant to support coaching and player development. It is not a substitute for medical advice, emergency response, or a guarantee of athletic results.

How the product works

Players upload or review tennis footage, select the stroke or session context, and receive a report that organizes movement quality, tactical context, and recommended next actions. Paid plans can add saved reports, dashboards, drill access, and coach-oriented workflows.
