About

The fastest way from 'what went wrong' to 'what to do next.'

SmartSwing AI was built because most tennis players finish every session not knowing what actually cost them points — or how to fix it before the next one. We built the AI film room that levels the playing field between coached and uncoached athletes.

What we built it to solve

  • The gap between filming a stroke and understanding what's wrong with it — closed to 60 seconds.
  • The translation problem between biomechanics data and real coaching language — solved.
  • The 'progress feels invisible' problem — fixed with session-over-session tracking.
  • The privacy and billing complexity — handled so athletes can focus on tennis.

Built for every player who's serious about getting better

SmartSwing AI is for junior athletes building habits, adult players who want clarity between lessons, coaches who need faster triage, and programs that want measurable results across their full roster — without custom software.

The product is meant to support coaching and player development. It is not a substitute for medical advice, emergency response, or a guarantee of athletic results.

This is how it works

Upload one video. Get a biomechanics report with your top 3 priority fixes, matching drills, and tactical guidance. Paid plans add report history, coaching workflows, drill libraries, and progress tracking.
