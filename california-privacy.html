California

California residents can review rights and submit privacy requests here.

This page is intended to help California residents understand available rights relating to access, deletion, correction, and certain opt-out choices under applicable California privacy law.

Available choices

  • Request access to categories or specific pieces of personal information, where required by law.
  • Request deletion or correction of eligible personal information.
  • Ask about categories of data collected, disclosed, or shared for business purposes.
  • Use available preference controls or contact SmartSwing AI to exercise applicable opt-out rights.

How to submit a request

Use the contact page and select a privacy-related topic. Include your full name, email address, the right you want to exercise, and enough detail for SmartSwing AI to verify and respond to your request.

Important notes

Some information may need to be retained for security, account maintenance, payment records, fraud prevention, or legal compliance. SmartSwing AI will not discriminate against users for exercising applicable privacy rights.

Effective date: March 25, 2026
