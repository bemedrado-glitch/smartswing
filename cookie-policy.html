Cookies

Cookie and storage controls should be understandable, not hidden.

This Cookie Policy explains how SmartSwing AI may use cookies, local storage, pixels, analytics tools, and similar technologies on the public website and logged-in experience.

Categories

  • Strictly necessary items that support login state, security, and core service functions.
  • Analytics items that help measure performance, errors, and product usage patterns.
  • Preference items that remember choices such as consent or user experience settings.
  • Marketing or personalization tools only where applicable and permitted by law.

Consent and region-specific use

Consent expectations can vary by region. Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland may see or require consent choices before certain non-essential technologies are used. California residents may also have additional rights relating to sharing, advertising, or opt-out controls. Brazilian users may also have consent-related and information rights under the LGPD depending on the cookie or processing context.

Managing choices

You can manage cookies through browser settings, delete stored data, or use the cookie banner when available. Blocking some technologies may affect sign-in persistence, analytics, or personalized product behavior. SmartSwing AI aims to avoid loading non-essential analytics on browsers that have not recorded analytics consent through the available preference flow.
