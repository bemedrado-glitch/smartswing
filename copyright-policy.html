Copyright

Respect original content and report problems with enough detail to investigate them.

SmartSwing AI respects copyright and expects users to upload or share only content they have the right to use. This policy describes how to report allegedly infringing material and how SmartSwing AI may respond.

Notice expectations

  • Identify the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed.
  • Identify the material you want reviewed with enough specificity to locate it.
  • Provide your name, contact information, and a good-faith explanation of the claim.
  • Confirm that the information in your notice is accurate to the best of your knowledge.

How SmartSwing AI may respond

Depending on the claim, SmartSwing AI may remove or restrict access to content, contact the reporting party or the affected user, request more detail, or take further account-level action for repeated infringement or misuse.

Where to send notices

Use the contact page and select a copyright-related topic. Include URLs, screenshots, dates, and enough detail to identify the material at issue.

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Copyright support

Notices should be specific enough for SmartSwing AI to locate the material in question.
