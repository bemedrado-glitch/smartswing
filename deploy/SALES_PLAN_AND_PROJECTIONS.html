3. Platform Gaps — What Needs to Be Built Action Required

Based on a full audit of the platform vs. competitive standards and conversion requirements. Items marked CRITICAL block revenue. Items marked HIGH reduce churn. Items marked GROWTH accelerate expansion.

✓ Complete — Live

Email Onboarding Sequence

9 transactional templates live via Resend (mail.smartswingai.com). Covers: welcome, analysis warning, paywall hit, paywall follow-up 3d & 7d, payment success, win-back 7d & 21d, referral bonus. Cron job sends win-back emails daily at 10:00 UTC. Bounce/complaint webhook protects deliverability.

Critical — Pre-Launch

Mobile In-Browser Camera Capture

Currently upload-only. 85% of users are on mobile. Users need to point and record directly from the browser without switching apps. Web Camera API supports this — needs implementation.

Critical — Pre-Launch

Shareable Report Link

Users can't share their biomechanics report with their coach via a link. This is the #1 B2B viral mechanism. A coach who receives a shared report becomes a discovery channel for 20+ new users.

Critical — Pre-Launch

Progress Timeline View

No way to compare analyses across sessions. "See how far you've come" is the strongest retention hook in all of fitness SaaS. Users need a timeline of their SmartSwing Score over weeks and months.

✓ Complete — Live

Referral Program

"Give 2 free analyses, get 2 free analyses." refer-friends.html page is live. Referral bonus email template built and wired. Referrals table in Supabase tracking referrer-to-friend conversions.

High — Month 1–3

Before / After Comparison View

Side-by-side video and metrics comparison between two analyses. The strongest upgrade driver: users want to see their improvement on the paid plan, not just in their memory.

✓ Complete — Live

Stripe Billing Portal

Self-service billing portal live via api/create-billing-portal-session.js. Users can manage, switch, pause, or cancel their subscription directly from Settings → Billing. Zero support tickets for billing changes.

High — Month 1–3

Demo Video on Homepage

No product walkthrough video on index.html. A 90-second demo video on the homepage increases trial sign-up conversion by 25–40%. This is the highest ROI single asset to create.

B2B — Month 3–6

Club / Academy Bulk Portal

Clubs need a single dashboard to manage all their members, assign drills, and review progress. This unlocks the $199–$699/mo B2B plans. Without it, clubs can't justify the premium.

B2B — Month 3–6

White-Label Coach Reports

Coaches on the Academy plan need reports with their logo and branding to share with students and parents. This is a strong retention lock-in — coaches build their practice around it.

Growth — Month 6–12

Shareable Score Cards (Social)

An Instagram/TikTok-ready image showing SmartSwing Score and top 3 improvements. Shareable cards create organic viral growth. Duolingo's streak cards drove 30% of their organic installs.

Growth — Month 6–12

Coach Marketplace

Players want to be matched with USPTA/PTR coaches. Coaches want to find students. A marketplace creates a new revenue line (10–15% commission) and makes SmartSwing the hub of the player-coach relationship.
