FAQ

Common questions.

What does SmartSwing AI analyze in a tennis video?
SmartSwing AI analyzes biomechanics including stroke mechanics, hip rotation, contact point, footwork patterns, and follow-through. It identifies your top technical issues and maps them to drills and tactical adjustments — in plain language you can act on immediately.
Does SmartSwing AI work for coaches?
Yes. Coaches get a dedicated dashboard to review player reports, assign drills, add notes, and track progress over time. The coach workflow is fully separate from the player view so each role only sees what it needs.
Do I need to download an app to use SmartSwing AI?
No. SmartSwing AI is fully web-based. Upload a video from any browser on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop — with no app download required.
How fast does SmartSwing AI generate a report?
Most reports are ready within 60 seconds of uploading a video. You get your top 3 priority fixes, matching drill recommendations, and tactical guidance without waiting.
Does SmartSwing AI work for pickleball?
Yes. SmartSwing AI supports both tennis and pickleball swing analysis, with sport-specific biomechanics scoring calibrated for each game's unique mechanics and player needs.
How is the scoring adjusted for different player levels?
The scoring engine adjusts for level, age, and gender so a beginner's report is benchmarked differently from an advanced player's. This prevents discouraging output and makes progress feel meaningful at every stage.
