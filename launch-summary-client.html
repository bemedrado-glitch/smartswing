Legal review Policy pages should be reviewed by counsel before broader public rollout.
Final manual browser QA One last pass should be done on real desktop and mobile devices before launch.

Recommended Launch Sequence

Step 1: Finish live setup Connect production Supabase and Stripe values and verify real account creation and payment events.
Step 2: Run a controlled soft launch Invite a limited group of players, coaches, or trusted testers and collect feedback before spending on traffic.
Step 3: Tighten based on behavior Use real friction points from sign-up, plan selection, onboarding, and checkout to refine the experience.
Step 4: Expand once production is proven Start broader promotion only after live billing, persistence, and support processes are confirmed.

Practical Recommendation

If the goal is to start getting feedback quickly, do not wait for perfect. Finish live auth and billing setup, do one final device check, and then begin with a limited early-access group.

If the goal is a wider public launch or paid acquisition, more caution is warranted. That should wait until the live production environment is fully verified and legal review is complete.

Good next move Launch to a small invited audience and learn from real users.
Not yet advised Broad announcement, paid ads, or scaled public acquisition before live setup is fully confirmed.
