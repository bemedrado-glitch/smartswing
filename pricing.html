What counts as one review?

Each generated report counts as one review. Opening the analyzer alone does not consume a credit.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans can be canceled anytime. Yearly plans can also be canceled anytime and remain active through the end of the paid annual term.

Why can't Starter save reports?

Starter is designed to create the first success moment. Save, print, and export unlock on paid plans.

Where do drills and tactics unlock?

Pro and Tournament Pro unlock the full drill and tactics library inside reports and the library page.
